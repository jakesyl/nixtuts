NixTuts started from an IRC channel at spotchat.org. Most of us participated in a couple of help channels, namely the Linux Mint help channels at Spotchat. We decided to create a place where we could help newbies by inviting them to this place and teaching them basic Linux knowledge.

Eventually, this involved into a website, when we thought: why not just create a website and link there?

From then on, we were commited to creating quality tutorials for the *nix community.

You're welcome to join us! Just visit us at any of our IRC channels as stated below.
